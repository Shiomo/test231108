ATTENTION

English


①
On the day of the event, entrance lanes will be set up within the venue grounds, so participants can’t meet up in the vicinity of the venue. Please arrange to meet at another location in advance.

②
Entry may be refused to those who are drunk or
who do not follow staff instructions and precautions.

③
To avoid congestion in the venue, as a general rule,
only those eligible to the “20-Year-old Gathering” and invited guests are allowed entrance.
However, if you require an assistance by a caregiver, please ask at the reception desk.

④
Please use public transportation to reach the venue.
The roads surrounding the venue will be very congested,
so please refrain from using a car (including sending-off and dropping-off by a car).
To prevent traffic accidents, parking near the venue is strictly prohibited.
Free shuttle buses will be provided on the day of the ceremony.

⑤
Smoking is prohibited inside the premises.

⑥
Bringing alcoholic beverages to the premises is strictly prohibited.

⑦
Depending on the future social situation, we may have to cancel the event at short notice.
Please check the Ota City’s official website, X (formerly Twitter), and the website of the “20-Year-old Gathering”
Steering Committee before coming to the venue to confirm whether or not the event will be held.
Please also note that we will not compensate for cancellation of the Haregi gown rental, etc.
